Our Story

We're on a Mission to Save the High Street

Independent businesses are the heartbeat of every town. We're building the tools to help them thrive.

Why We Started Rezvo

Big platforms take 20-30% commission from small businesses that can barely afford it. We started Rezvo because we believed there was a better way.

A platform that puts the business owner first — their brand, their customers, their revenue.

We watched talented barbers, salon owners, and restaurant managers work 60-hour weeks only to see a third of their digital revenue disappear to faceless tech companies. It didn't sit right with us.

So we built something different. A platform where you keep 100% of what you earn, and your brand stays front and center.

Our Core Values

Everything we build is guided by these principles.

Zero Commission

We never take a cut of your earnings. Your revenue is yours to keep, invest, and grow your business with.

Your Brand First

Your name on everything, not ours. Full white-label customization so clients see your business, not a third-party platform.

Built for Real People

No technical skills needed, ever. We design for business owners, not software engineers. Simple, intuitive, powerful.

Meet the Founder

The person behind the mission.

Alex Morrison

Founder & CEO

After spending 10 years working with independent businesses across the UK, Alex saw firsthand how predatory commission models were strangling small business growth. He founded Rezvo in 2025 with a simple mission: give business owners the tools they deserve without taking a slice of their hard-earned revenue.

"Every business owner should have access to world-class technology without sacrificing their profit margins. That's what we're building at Rezvo."
