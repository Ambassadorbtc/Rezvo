Frequently Asked Questions

Got Questions?

Everything you need to know about our referral programme

What plans qualify?

Any paid plan qualifies — Starter, Growth, or Scale. Both you and your referral need to be on a paid subscription to receive the 50% discount. Free trials don't count until they convert to paid.

Is it really forever?

Yes. As long as both accounts stay active on paid plans, the 50% discount stays. There's no expiration date, no fine print, no catch. This isn't a 3-month promo — it's a permanent benefit for helping us grow.

Can I refer more than one business?

Absolutely! Each successful referral locks in 50% off for both you and your referral. Refer 5 businesses, help 5 friends save, and you keep your discount as long as at least one referral stays active. The more you refer, the more businesses you help.

What if my referral cancels?

Your discount stays. You earned it by bringing them to Rezvo, and it's yours to keep. If you have multiple active referrals, your discount remains as long as at least one of them stays subscribed. We reward loyalty.

When does the discount start?

The moment your referral's free trial ends and they convert to a paid plan, both of your accounts automatically switch to 50% off. You'll see the discount reflected on your next billing cycle.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes! Your 50% discount applies to whichever plan you're on. Switch from Starter to Growth? You'll pay 50% of the Growth price. Downgrade from Scale to Growth? Same deal — 50% off your new plan.
